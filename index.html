about us why choose us

what makes our coffee special!

The Coffee Club, is India’s favourite hangout for coffee and conversations. Popularly known as JOJO Cafe, we strive to provide the best experience to our guests. Our coffees are sourced from thousands of small coffee planters, who made us who we are today and we're glad to be a part of their lives.The youth and the young at heart love our cafe, and it continues to be one of the most happening places in the city. JOJO Cafe to the youth is a “hangout” spot where they meet people, make conversations, and have a whole lot of fun over steaming cups of great coffee.It's been an exciting journey since then to becoming the largest organised retail cafe chain in the city.
